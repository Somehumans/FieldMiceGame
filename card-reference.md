## Card Design Notes

- **Number cards**: Bold number in top-left and bottom-right corners
- **Trump cards**: Gold/holographic border to distinguish from number cards, card name at top, tiny effect text at bottom
- **All cards**: Thick black outlines, flat colors with minimal shading, same border/frame shape
- **Card back**: Sleeping rat curled up in a closed flower bud, dark background, "21" watermark
- **Style**: Fun 2D illustrated, similar to a colorful indie card game
- **Dimensions**: Recommend drawing at 750x1050px (standard card ratio 5:7)
