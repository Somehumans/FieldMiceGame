Card Art Reference Guide -- Draw your own versions in a fun 2D illustrated style

Number Cards (1 - 11)

1
Mouse on a Stick with Blueberry
A single field mouse perched on top of a stick, hugging a blueberry. Tiny, simple, peaceful.
Minimal. One subject, one object. The quietest card in the deck.
Cool blue / soft green
2
Mouse Climbing a Yellow Tulip
Harvest mouse climbing a yellow tulip stem, peeking out from behind the leaf. Alert, curious.
One flower, slightly more going on. Still clean and simple.
Yellow / green
3
Two Mice on a Drooping Tulip
Two mice on a drooping yellow tulip -- one perched on the stem, the other peeking out from inside the flower. The flower bends under their weight.
First card with two subjects. Playful, a little mischievous.
Yellow / warm green
4
Mouse Hanging from Buttercup Branches
Mouse dangling by its tail from a branching stem with yellow buttercups. Mid-swing, acrobatic.
Dynamic movement. Multiple flowers and branches. More action than earlier cards.
Green / yellow
5
Dormouse Laughing on Yarrow
Dormouse sitting on top of a white yarrow flower cluster, mouth open in a laugh/yawn, paws spread wide. Pure joy.
The midpoint card. Big personality. This will be the fan favorite.
White / warm green / orange fur
6
Mouse Climbing Tall Buttercup Stems
Harvest mouse climbing tall buttercup stems against a bright blue sky. Multiple branching stems and flowers, scaling upward.
Height, ambition, more environment visible. The scene feels bigger.
Blue sky / yellow / green
7
Mouse Among Billy Buttons (Dark)
Mouse perched among a forest of golden billy button / craspedia flowers against a black background. Lots of round golden orbs.
Mood shift. Dark backdrop, golden spheres. Visual density increasing.
Gold / black / dark
8
Mouse on Cherry Blossom Branch
Mouse sitting on a long cherry blossom branch with delicate white flowers stretching across the full width. Serene and elegant.
Wide, graceful composition. Thorny branch with beauty. Calm before the storm.
Soft green / white / pink tint
9
Two Mice Meeting on Daffodils
Two mice face-to-face on daffodil stems, touching noses/paws. Big dramatic yellow flowers, warm moody background.
Intimate, emotional. Large flowers dominate. High visual weight.
Deep yellow / warm brown
10
Three Mice on Hawthorn Berries
Three mice scrambling up a hawthorn branch loaded with red berries. Tails tangling everywhere. Crowded and chaotic.
The busiest card. Three subjects, lots of berries, autumn leaves. Controlled chaos.
Red berries / autumn green / brown
11
Three Mice on Thistle Heads
Three mice each sitting atop their own thistle/scabious seed head, standing tall like sentinels against a dreamy blue meadow backdrop.
The crown card. Regal, panoramic. Tiny kings surveying their kingdom.
Blue / green meadow / earth tones

Design Notes

  • Number cards: Bold number in top-left and bottom-right corners
  • Trump cards: Gold/holographic border to distinguish from number cards, card name at top, tiny effect text at bottom
  • All cards: Thick black outlines, flat colors with minimal shading, same border/frame shape
  • Card back: Sleeping rat curled up in a closed flower bud, dark background, "21" watermark
  • Style: Fun 2D illustrated, chunky bold typography, splatter/spray paint textures
  • Dimensions: Recommend drawing at 750 x 1050px (standard card ratio 5:7)
  • File format: Save as PNG with transparency, name them: card-1.png through card-11.png, trump-plus1.png, trump-steal.png, etc.
  • Card back file: card-back.png
